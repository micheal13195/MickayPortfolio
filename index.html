    About Me

    A skilled Data Analyst with a focus on Data Modelling and Machine Learning, I leverage expertise in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Big Data Analytics to drive significant financial growth and empower clients in making informed investment decisions. My experience spans over two years at TradeVerse Nig. Ltd., where I achieved over 200% annual profits through comprehensive financial analysis and strategic affiliations with major crypto exchanges like Bybit, BingX, and Binance Africa. Additionally, my strong background in Biochemistry and experience in the pharmaceutical industry have honed my analytical skills, particularly in ensuring product safety and quality. I hold a B.Sc. in Biochemistry from Obafemi Awolowo University and have completed advanced training in Natural Language Processing, Software Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence. My leadership roles include serving as a Cryptocurrency Community Manager and representing students in the Nigeria Society of Biochemistry Students. I am passionate about using data-driven insights to empower others and achieve financial freedom.
